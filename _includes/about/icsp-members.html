ICSP Members

Chaired by the CSOTUS, the ICSP is comprised of 26 other members. Pursuant to the Paperwork Reduction Act of 1995 (PRA), all 13 heads of the principal statistical agencies are members. Pursuant to the Evidence Act, all 24 Statistical Officials are also members. Eleven of the Statistical Officials are also heads of principal statistical agencies or units.
