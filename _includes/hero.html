Welcome to StatsPolicy.gov

Welcome to StatsPolicy.gov, the home of the U.S. Federal statistical system. StatsPolicy.gov brings together important information about our decentralized, yet increasingly interwoven system, including about its leadership, priorities, and activities across the Federal Government. Key governing laws and policies, community news and events, and other resources are also provided.
